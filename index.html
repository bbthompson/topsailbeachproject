Welcome to Topsail Beach

Located in Pender County, NC, the town of Topsail Beach is a welcoming town known for its family friendly beaches and sea turtle conservation effort. No high rise development is allowed in the town, and conservation of the island and beaches are the community’s highest priority. Topsail Beach is located at the southern end of North Carolina’s 26-mile long Topsail Island. The town boasts many restaurants and local shops. Beaches offer opportunities for relaxing, swimming, fishing, surfing, and sand castle building. Water adventurers will enjoy convenient access via the town's marina. Annual festivals are hosted by the community, and Wilmington is a less than hour drive away. We hope you come visit us soon!
